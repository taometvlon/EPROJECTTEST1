Job Description

We are looking for non-specialist doctors who are interested in broad-based and clinical-focused work at Departments of Medicine, ICU, Emergency Medicine, Anaesthesia, Surgery and Orthopaedic Surgery.

About the Hospital Clinician career scheme

The Hospital Clinician scheme is a comprehensive and structured long-term career path for doctors who aspire to take on higher duties and wider responsibilities in a hospital setting. Hospital Clinicians will anchor inpatient care services in acute and subacute public hospitals (including Community Hospitals), in a role complementary to specialists. Through development of broad-based as well as clinical focused skills, HCs will form the 'backbone' of the hospital's medical care resource, working in teams with Specialist doctors, Nurses and Allied Health Professionals.

Who should apply?

  • MOPEX and other locally trained doctors who have completed at least 3 years of clinical and relevant experiences.
  • Enjoys broad based work in a variety of different settings in the public sector.
  • Aspires to take on clinical and admin leadership roles.

Job Description

The Renal Coordinator is responsible in educating mild chronic renal failure patients and end stage renal failure patients in self-care and teaching patients at risk of developing renal failure on retardation or prevention of the disease. The role involves:
  • Providing patient education for chronic kidney disease patients
  • Presenting treatment options for moderate to end stage renal failure patients
  • Providing financial counselling for new dialysis patients or patients who are going to initiate dialysis
  • Providing counselling service

  • Coordinating of dialysis plan

  • Maintaining and updating of CKD (Chronic Kidney Disease) and ESRD (End Stage Renal Disease) Registry
